I’m a lover of minivans, dad, and a product designer @tradesy.

To me, product design is not about making beautiful things. Effective product design is about creating something that you truly believe in; something human beings will, and want to, use; and making it better and better everyday.

The word beautiful permeates the digital product world unfortunately; and it does nothing to help me, nor anyone else, derive any significant value about said product.
